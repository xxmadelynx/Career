    Some of the tasks that a Forensic Scientist does would be to:

  • Investigate the body/crime scene
  • Collect evidence
  • Examine the evidence
  • Figure out the cause of death

    • A Forensic Scientist is a specialist in a field of science who devotes their knowledge to the purposes of law. This means that they play an important role in the criminal justice system. They help analyze and collect evidence from the crime scene in order to figure out the cause of death. The education required is that is that you should have a four year undergraduate degree in a field of chemistry, biology, or anything related to science. Another option you can do is go for a master's degree. People who are laboratory experienced earn more up to $3,000 per month. Forensic Scientist usually earn annual salaries between $35,000 and $50,000.

    A Forensic Scientist helps to investigate any crime scene in order to figure out who, when, and where everything occured.
